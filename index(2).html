Established in 2002, DC Livery is headquartered in Washington, D.C., with offices in New York and a network of well-qualified partners in all major cities worldwide. We take pride in maintaining the highest quality of service and professionalism while offering only late-model vehicles and a versatile fleet of multiple vehicles from sedans to coaches and everything in between. Moreover, we offer competitive pricing and guarantee prompt services worldwide.

We are focused on building a lifelong relationship with our clients by providing reliable, courteous, professional and secure ground transportation services. Whether it's a multi-vehicle road show, a sedan service for airport transfer, a limousine for corporate/private events, a wedding or prom or a night on the town, you or your clients can enjoy the luxury and comfort of our meticulously maintained fleets and competitive rates.


We have been in business for over a decade. During that time, we have perfected processes to ensure the service experience is smooth from start to finish and further we strive to improve our performance every day. The hard work put in by our team and frequent referrals by our loyal clients keep our company fueled for success.


Our office staff is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, to assist you with your ground travel plans. Call us today for more information, availability of vehicles, current special offers and reservations.
